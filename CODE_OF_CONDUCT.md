# Code of Conduct

We expect all project contributors to be respectful and considerate to one another. Please follow these guidelines:

- Be civil and kind; avoid abusive or insulting language.
- Do not engage in discrimination, harassment, or threats.
- Provide constructive feedback and assume good faith.

Reports of unacceptable behavior will be taken seriously. If you experience or witness issues, contact the maintainers or follow the guidance in `SECURITY.md`.

This document is based on common community standards for open source projects.
